About Rukam Sitara

Rukam Sitara, based in New Delhi, is a venture capital firm dedicated to early-stage technology startups in India. Established in 2023 by Archana Jahagirdar, we're more than just investors. We're partners, mentors, and collaborators, committed to capturing value in India's dynamic tech landscape.

process

Our unique approach

01

Discovery

Non-conformist thinking: at Rukam Sitara, we don't follow the crowd. We craft our own thesis about a sector or market, driven by a vision to identify the next tech giants.

02

Research

Understanding the landscape: at Rukam Sitara, we delve into the intricate landscape of our chosen sectors. By gaining deep insights into the players and their dynamics, we ensure that our Investment decisions are well-informed, strategic, and poised for success.

03

Investment

Finding winners: our mission goes beyond mere financial backing. We dig deep, seeking startups with solid technical foundations, proven revenue models, and immense growth potential. We're here to fuel growth and innovation, believing in their potential to shape the future.
